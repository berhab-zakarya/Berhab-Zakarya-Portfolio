# I Wright Go (For English Student)
iWrightGo app
In four sections:
iWriting Class: a virtual platform where the teacher and students can share any sort of files or links to extend their writing classes outside the formal classroom.
iSentence Writing: a further source of understanding that encompasses all the lessons associated with sentence writing in English.. Those are related to grammar, capitalization, punctuation, style, spelling of words (Orthography) and vocabulary
iWrite page: here is a space for writing and self editing. It is worth mentioning that, unlike the common checkers, this page SOLELY hints the errors in writing without correcting them. This is an endeavor to keep the students' challenge to identify the type of errors she/he makes and edit them by themselves
iWordfinder: a simplified wordfinder dictionary that helps the student to check out the meaning and the spelling of words. According to, this may aid in widening students knowledge of vocabulary and the proper way of using it
